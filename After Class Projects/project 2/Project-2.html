Delhi Public School, Greater Noida

About Us

Delhi Public School Greater Noida, one of the core schools under the DPS Society, offers a holistic and inclusive education from Nursery to Grade XII.Set in a lush 15-acre campus, the school emphasizes academic excellence while fostering 21st-century skills like communication, creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration. It aims to nurture confident, compassionate, and well-rounded individuals. through a supportive environment that values mutual respect, personal growth, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision

To nurture globally conscious leaders through academic excellence and holistic development. We envision a dynamic learning environment where intellectual curiosity flourishes alongside alongside moral integrity, preparing students to navigate an evolving world with confidence and compassion. At DPS Greater Noida, every child discovers their unique potential and develops a lifelong passion for learning, emerging as responsible global citizens ready to transform tomorrow."Excellence in Education, Excellence in Life"

Our Mission

"To provide each student with diverse education in a safe environment that promotes high values, self-discipline and motivated excellence in learning."

Student Enrollment

Currently, over 4,000 students are enrolled across all grades, making us one of the most vibrant learning communities in the region.
